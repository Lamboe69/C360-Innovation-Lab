Who We Are

Building Uganda's
Innovation Ecosystem

C360 Innovation Lab is a youth-driven platform connecting students, mentors, and partners to foster creativity, career growth, and enterprise across Uganda.

01 / PROGRAMS
💡

Innovation & Creativity

We run structured innovation clubs where young people tackle real-world challenges using design thinking, technology, and creative problem-solving at scale.

Explore Programs →
02 / MENTORSHIP
🎯

Career Guidance

Our mentorship network connects youth with experienced professionals who provide direction, skills training, and clear pathways into meaningful and impactful careers.

Meet Mentors →
03 / ENTERPRISE
🚀

Enterprise Development

From idea to launch — we support young entrepreneurs through incubation, financial literacy training, and access to our thriving network of business partners.

Start Your Journey →

🔬

Research & Innovation

We foster a culture of critical thinking by supporting youth-led projects, innovation competitions, and challenges that push young Ugandans to solve real problems.

Financial Literacy
