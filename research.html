🔬  Research

Research, Innovation & Critical Thinking

We foster a culture of research and critical thinking by supporting youth-led projects, competitions, and innovation challenges across Uganda.

What We Do
Our Research Pillars
📋

Youth-Led Projects

We support young researchers and innovators in designing, executing, and publishing projects that address real challenges in their communities and beyond.

🏆

Innovation Competitions

Regular competitions challenge youth to pitch ideas, build prototypes, and present solutions — with prizes, recognition, and pathways into our incubation program.

🧠

Innovation Challenges

Themed challenges issued by C360 and our partners invite youth to tackle specific problems in agriculture, health, education, and climate — with mentorship throughout.
