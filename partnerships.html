Youth Innovation Seed Grant

A startup grant of up to UGX 5,000,000 for young innovators with a viable idea or early-stage project. Funds can be used for prototyping, materials, and initial operations.

👤 Ages 15–25📅 Rolling Applications💰 Up to UGX 5M

School Club Development Grant

Supports primary and secondary schools in establishing or strengthening C360 Innovation Clubs. Covers training materials, equipment, and club leader stipends for one academic year.

🏫 Schools Only📅 Deadline: 31 Aug 2025💰 Up to UGX 8M
Apply Now →

Community Enterprise Grant

Designed for community-based groups and cooperatives looking to launch or scale a social enterprise. Includes both funding and a 3-month business mentorship package.
