💰  Financial Literacy

Financial Literacy & Business Training

We promote financial literacy, savings culture, and business training for winning ideas from C360 Clubs — equipping young entrepreneurs with the skills to build real businesses and contribute directly to Uganda's economic development.

Core Areas
What We Teach
📈

Financial Literacy

We equip young people with the knowledge to understand money, manage personal finances, avoid debt traps, and make informed financial decisions in everyday life and business.

🤑

Savings & Investment

Youth learn the power of saving early, how to set financial goals, and how to explore simple investment options — from savings groups to mobile money and beyond.

📄

Business Planning

We guide young entrepreneurs through building solid business plans — covering market research, pricing, cash flow, and pitching to investors and grant committees.

Learn at Your Own Pace
Training Modules
💰

Money Basics & Budgeting

Understand income, expenses, and how to create a personal or household budget that works. Includes practical worksheets and real-life scenarios.

🕐 3 Hours
🤑

Savings Culture & Goal Setting

Learn how to build a consistent savings habit, set short and long-term financial goals, and use tools like VSLAs and mobile savings accounts.

🕐 2.5 Hours
📄

Writing a Business Plan

A step-by-step guide to writing a fundable business plan — from executive summary to financial projections. Includes a downloadable template.

🕐 4 Hours
🚀

Pitching & Accessing Finance

Master the art of pitching your business idea to investors, banks, and grant committees. Covers storytelling, financials, and handling tough questions.

🕐 3.5 Hours
Exclusively for Club Winners
The Winners Training Pathway
🏆

Won a C360 Club Competition? This is your next step.

The Winners Training Pathway is a structured business training program reserved for competition winners and top performers from C360 Clubs. It takes you from a winning idea to a viable, fundable business — with hands-on coaching, financial tools, and direct access to our incubation network.

🏆

Win & Qualify

Place in a C360 club competition or be nominated by your club leader as a top performer with a viable idea.

📚

Business Bootcamp

Complete a 4-week intensive covering financial literacy, business modelling, pricing, and cash flow management.

🤝

1-on-1 Coaching

Get paired with a dedicated business coach who reviews your plan, challenges your assumptions, and prepares you to pitch.

🚀

Pitch & Launch

Present your business to a panel of investors and partners. Top pitches receive seed funding and incubation support.
